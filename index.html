About Me

Jubayer Ahmed Sajid is a senior Aerospace Engineering student at Aviation and Aerospace University, Bangladesh, specializing in CAD, additive manufacturing, and ANSYS-based advanced simulations. A National CAD Competition Champion, he has contributed to major international aerospace competitions, including AIAA Design/Build/Fly and the VFS Design Competition. His research focuses on composites, structural optimization, bird strike analysis, and advanced simulation methods combined with Machine Learning. He has published in MDPI Designs, has five conference papers accepted at IOCAE 2026, and has also submitted journal papers on structural FEA and machine learning-based bird strike analysis. Guided by Dr. Saiaf Bin Rayhan and Dr. Ivan Grgic, he is building a strong profile as an emerging aerospace researcher and engineer.
